# HealthCare
Medical Service at your doorstep and your medical records

Our app promotes periodical regular check ups by reminding the users. To help the user, we connect the user with a
practioner who'll then come your home and generating a report. 

Our app has 3 activities: 
1. One contains medical history of a patient.
2. One shows the nearby clinics you can contact to get your regular check up.
3. One shows your family doctors whom you can consult with your reports.
